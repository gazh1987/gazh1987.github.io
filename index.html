About Me


Welcome to my portfolio website. My name is Gary Healy. I am currently in my fourth and final year at Dublin Institute of Technology, studying a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science. I am specializing in Applied Intelligence, Geographical Information Systems and Rich Web Applications. I am also currently working on my fourth year project, "Rendezvous Friend Finder", which is a mobile Geographical Information System for Android. The app allows friends to track each others GPS coordinates and uses a compass an a map to aid navigation.

My main interests in computing are Geographical Information Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Games Development and Web Development. Outside of computing, I am intersted in music, art and film.

Thank you for visiting the site!
